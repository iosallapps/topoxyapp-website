FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Which countries are supported?

Romania (Stereo 70), Poland (PUWG 1992), Czechia (S-JTSK Křovák), Slovakia (S-JTSK Křovák), Italy (Gauss-Boaga zone 2 with UTM 32N/33N alternates) and Germany (ETRS89 / UTM 32N with UTM 33N for east of 12°E). The active country is set during onboarding and changeable from Settings.

How accurate is the conversion?

Sub-centimetre for PL/CZ/SK/DE (libproj with national grids), sub-metre for RO (hand-rolled Stereo 70 Helmert math), and engineering ±1–3 m for IT (NTv2 grid not yet bundled — accuracy disclaimer is shown in the app before purchase).

Does it replace a licensed surveyor?

No. TopoXY is an engineering-grade tool for surveying professionals — it accelerates daily fieldwork, but official cadastral submissions still require a licensed surveyor under each country's regulations (RO Law 7/1996, DE ÖbVI, IT Collegio dei Geometri, etc.).

Does it work offline?

Coordinate conversion is fully on-device. Online services (cadastre, orthophoto, elevation lookup, OpenStreetMap features) require connectivity. The app is honest about this — you'll see which features need signal.

What data do you collect?

Coordinates and calculations stay on your device. We collect anonymous analytics and crash reports via Firebase only after GDPR consent (UMP). No personally identifiable info. See our Privacy Policy for the full processor list.

How do I cancel my subscription?

On your iPhone: Settings → [Your Apple ID] → Subscriptions → TopoXY Pro → Cancel. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.
