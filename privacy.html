Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TopoXY. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how TopoXY handles your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Coordinates: When you enter coordinates for conversion, these are processed locally on your device and are not stored on our servers.
  • Saved Collections: Coordinate collections you save are stored locally on your device using iOS storage.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use TopoXY, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, iOS version, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Crash Reports: Technical information when the app crashes to help us fix bugs.

2.3 Location Data

TopoXY may request access to your device's location to show your current position on the map, auto-fill coordinates based on your location, and calculate distances. Location access is optional and can be denied or revoked at any time in iOS Settings.

3. Third-Party Services

TopoXY integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Mapbox: For elevation data and base maps
  • ANCPI: For Romanian ortophoto imagery
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management

4. Data Storage

Most data generated by TopoXY is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain servers that store your coordinates or personal data.

5. Your Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Delete Local Data: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Revoke Location Access: Deny location permission in iOS Settings
  • Limit Ad Tracking: Enable "Limit Ad Tracking" in iOS Settings
