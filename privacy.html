Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2025

Effective Date: February 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TopoXY ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how TopoXY handles your data when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Our Privacy Commitment: TopoXY processes coordinate conversions and terrain analysis locally on your device whenever possible. We minimize data collection and only collect what's necessary to provide and improve our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Coordinates: When you enter coordinates for conversion, these are processed locally on your device and are not stored on our servers.
  • Saved Collections: Coordinate collections you save are stored locally on your device using iOS storage.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use TopoXY, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, iOS version, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Crash Reports: Technical information when the app crashes to help us fix bugs.

2.3 Location Data

TopoXY may request access to your device's location to:

  • Show your current position on the map
  • Auto-fill coordinates based on your location
  • Calculate distances from your location to points of interest

Location access is optional and you can deny or revoke it at any time in iOS Settings. The app functions without location access.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide coordinate conversion and terrain analysis services
  • Display relevant map data and satellite imagery
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand how users interact with features
  • Display advertisements (via Google AdMob)

4. Third-Party Services

TopoXY integrates with the following third-party services:

4.1 Mapbox

We use Mapbox for elevation data and base maps. When you request elevation data, your coordinates are sent to Mapbox servers. See Mapbox Privacy Policy.

4.2 ANCPI (Romanian Cadastre)

For Romanian ortophoto imagery, we connect to ANCPI's public WMS service. Coordinates are sent to retrieve satellite imagery tiles.

4.3 Google AdMob

TopoXY displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data

See Google Privacy Policy for more information.

4.4 Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns. This includes anonymous usage statistics. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

4.5 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat for subscription management. RevenueCat processes purchase data through Apple's App Store. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage

Most data generated by TopoXY is stored locally on your device:

  • Saved Coordinates: Stored locally using iOS UserDefaults
  • Preferences: App settings stored locally
  • Cache: Map tiles and imagery cached locally for performance

We do not maintain servers that store your coordinates or personal data. Coordinate processing happens either on-device or through third-party APIs (Mapbox, ANCPI).

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:

  • With third-party service providers as described above (Mapbox, ANCPI, AdMob)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or the safety of users

7. Children's Privacy

TopoXY is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at iosallapps@icloud.com.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data stored locally is protected by iOS security features
  • Network requests use HTTPS encryption
  • We minimize data collection to only what's necessary

9. Your Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Delete Local Data: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Revoke Location Access: Deny location permission in iOS Settings
  • Limit Ad Tracking: Enable "Limit Ad Tracking" in iOS Settings
  • Request Data Deletion: Contact us to request deletion of any data we may have

10. International Users

TopoXY is designed primarily for use in Romania but may be used internationally. If you use the app from outside Romania:

  • ANCPI ortophoto imagery is only available for Romania
  • Stereo70 projection is specific to Romania
  • Your data may be processed in different jurisdictions through third-party services

GDPR Compliance (EU Users): We comply with GDPR requirements. You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • The updated policy will be posted at https://topoxyapp.com/privacy.html

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how TopoXY handles your data, please contact us:

Email: iosallapps@icloud.com
Website: https://topoxyapp.com

We will respond to your inquiry within 7 business days.

13. Summary

What TopoXY Does:

  • ✅ Processes coordinates locally on your device
  • ✅ Stores your saved collections locally
  • ✅ Requests elevation data from Mapbox (coordinates sent)
  • ✅ Requests satellite imagery from ANCPI (coordinates sent)
  • ✅ Displays ads via Google AdMob
  • ✅ Collects anonymous analytics via Firebase

What TopoXY Does NOT Do:

  • ❌ Store your coordinates on our servers
  • ❌ Sell your personal information
  • ❌ Track your location without permission
  • ❌ Share your data with third parties beyond what's described

Your Privacy Matters.
TopoXY is designed with privacy in mind. Your coordinate data stays on your device.
